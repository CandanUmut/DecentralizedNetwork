Scanning opens an invite page (on your local network) that explains what this is and hands them the join file. Or download it yourself from join.json and send it any way you like — on their machine: timecoin-node run --config join.json. On the same wifi, nodes also find each other automatically.

What is this?

A community-run network: every member's computer keeps one shared, tamper-proof record of who helped whom. TimeCoin is created only by thanking someone — never bought or mined — and each member can give a limited amount of thanks per day. Messages are end-to-end encrypted and travel directly between members. No company, no server, no ads. See VISION.md in the repo for the full story and the honest limits.
